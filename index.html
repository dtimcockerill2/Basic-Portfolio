About Me

I am an up and coming web developer currently living in Charlotte, North Carolina. I am mainly interested in front end web development, but am building full stack skills through UNC-C's Coding Bootcamp.

Prior to pursuing web development, I had a fulfilling and diverse career in the food and beverage industry. I've spent time as a line cook, barista, baker, and sales representative for a major winery.

When I'm not learning to code or making coffee for people, I like to read, play with my dog, listen to hip hop, and hang out with my wife Sierra. I'm also a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and am constantly either training or recovering!
